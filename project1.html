Project 01

The AI Odyssey

A visual timeline portfolio artifact tracing the evolution of artificial intelligence from foundational milestones to the modern generative AI era.

Project Overview

This artifact presents the history of artificial intelligence as a professional visual narrative rather than a plain academic summary. The goal was to communicate major turning points in AI development in a format that is visually engaging, easy to follow, and suitable for a modern portfolio.

What This Artifact Shows

The project highlights how AI evolved across decades, from early symbolic systems and foundational theories to machine learning breakthroughs, deep learning acceleration, and the emergence of large language models and generative AI.

Tools & Methods

This work combined visual design thinking with structured research and academic communication. It was developed as a polished portfolio artifact rather than a text-heavy report.

Figma NotebookLM AI History Research Visual Communication

Key Takeaway

AI progress is not the result of one sudden breakthrough. It is the outcome of decades of accumulated research, computing advances, data growth, and new model architectures. Presenting that journey visually makes the field easier to understand and far more memorable.

Why This Matters

In a professional portfolio, presentation matters. This artifact demonstrates not only subject knowledge but also the ability to communicate technical ideas clearly, visually, and strategically for a broader audience.
