Project 01

The AI Odyssey

A visual research timeline tracing artificial intelligence from foundational milestones through expert systems, AI winters, deep learning breakthroughs, and the generative AI frontier.

Project Overview

This artifact presents the evolution of artificial intelligence as a polished visual narrative rather than a conventional report. It organizes major eras of AI into a structured timeline, making complex technical history easier to understand and more compelling to explore.

The project spans early milestones such as the Turing Test, Dartmouth Conference, and Perceptron, then moves through expert systems, the two AI winters, deep learning breakthroughs, transformers, foundation models, and the 2020–2025 generative AI explosion.

What This Artifact Shows

The timeline highlights the rise, decline, and rebirth of AI across multiple decades. It also profiles key researchers, major companies shaping modern AI, Narrow AI versus AGI, and the infrastructure shifts that made deep learning successful when it did.
