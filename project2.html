Project 02

AI-Powered Retail Support Transformation

A solution architecture case study showing how AI-powered NLP, real-time data integration, escalation design, and governance controls can transform retail support into a faster, more intelligent service model.

Project Overview

This project reimagines retail support as an intelligent service capability rather than a reactive help desk function. It addresses fragmented channels, repetitive inquiries, slow manual handling, and inconsistent customer experience by introducing an AI-powered support model.

The concept focuses on transforming post-purchase and service interactions through conversational AI, live backend connectivity, and structured decision flows that improve both operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

What This Artifact Shows

The artifact presents the full transformation story: retail support failure patterns, customer friction, intelligent support engine design, escalation flow, cloud architecture, context synchronization, intent translation, business impact, implementation roadmap, governance, and future-state opportunities.
